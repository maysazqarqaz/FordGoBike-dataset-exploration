Ford GoBike dataset exploration¶

by Maysa Qarqaz¶

Investigation Overview¶

The main goal of this investigation is to find the characterstics of bike users that could affect the trip duration. I will focus on these attributes: Member age and user type.

Dataset Overview¶

There are 183412 rides in the dataset with 16 features (duration, start time, end time, start and end stations info :id, name latitude and longitude, bike id, user type, member birth year, member gender and bike share for all trip). Some variables are numerical and other are nominal categorical variables. Time Frame of the data: February 2019

Trip duration distribution¶

Trip duration has a wide range of values, starting from 1 min and ending at about 1400 min, the distribution has a long tail with a peak at 10 min.
